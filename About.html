ABOUT THE CAFE

The Cafe was founded in Tabuk by UT students as their Graduation project. A group of four students created one of the coziest cafes ever, surrounded by their classmates and professors. Join us to an amazing experience.

"We use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Opening hours: everyday from 7am to 6pm.
